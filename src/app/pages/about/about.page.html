About What is this?

Essentials Anonymous is a platform where people can share experiences working and interacting with others during the COVID-19 pandemic.


Including information about your employer and job is optional, but feel free to name and shame as well as explain and proclaim!

 How Anonymous?

You don't even need an email address to sign in!

 How does it work?

Check out some posts and engage with others in the comments!


Submit a post of your own and even include an image if you want!


Most importantly you change the app colors and toggle dark mode!
